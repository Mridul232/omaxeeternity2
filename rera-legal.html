What is RERA?

The Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act, 2016 (RERA) is an act of the Parliament of India which seeks to protect home-buyers as well as help boost investments in the real estate industry. It requires all real estate projects to be registered with state-level Real Estate Regulatory Authorities.

Why RERA Registration Matters

  • Transparency: All project details including approved plans, layout, schedule, and financials are publicly available.
  • Accountability: Developers must adhere to promised timelines and specifications.
  • Buyer Protection: Legal recourse available through RERA tribunal for any disputes.
  • Fund Security: 70% of project funds must be kept in a separate escrow account.
  • No False Advertising: Developers can only advertise RERA-registered projects.

How to Verify on UP-RERA
