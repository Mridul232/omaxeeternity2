About Omaxe Eternity 2 Vrindavan

A thoughtfully planned township where modern living meets spiritual tranquility

Project Overview

Discover the vision behind Omaxe Eternity-2

Omaxe Eternity-2 is an integrated residential township spread across 49.1 acres in the sacred city of Vrindavan, Uttar Pradesh. This RERA-registered project (UPRERAPRJ141620/12/2025) represents a unique blend of modern infrastructure and spiritual heritage.

Located on the bustling Raal Road, Omaxe Eternity-2 is thoughtfully designed to complement the serene pace of Vrindavan while offering all the amenities essential for contemporary living. The project features well-planned residential plots in three premium sizes: 120 sq. yard, 150 sq. yard, and 200 sq. yard.

The township is meticulously planned with wide internal roads (45m and 30m master plan roads), dedicated green spaces, parks, and commercial zones, ensuring a complete and self-sufficient living experience for residents.
