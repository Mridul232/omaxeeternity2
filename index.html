Location: Raal Road, Vrindavan

Omaxe Eternity 2 enjoys an exceptional location in the spiritual capital of India. Situated on Raal Road, Vrindavan, the project is surrounded by temples, ashrams, and the sacred Yamuna River. Key location highlights include:

  • ~150 km from Delhi NCR via Yamuna Expressway (approx. 2 hrs drive)
  • ~13 km from Mathura Junction railway station
  • Close to ISKCON Temple, Banke Bihari Temple, Prem Mandir, Radha Raman Temple
  • ~85 km from Agra (Taj Mahal)
  • Near upcoming Jewar International Airport (Noida), boosting long-term value
  • Well-connected via NH-19 (Delhi-Agra Highway) and Yamuna Expressway

Omaxe Eternity 2 Amenities & Infrastructure

The amenities at Omaxe Eternity 2 are designed for a self-sufficient, modern lifestyle within a spiritual environment. The township features 45m and 30m wide master plan roads, landscaped green parks, a community temple complex, modern clubhouse, children's play areas, dedicated commercial zone with shops, EV charging stations, 24/7 gated security with CCTV, underground electrical cabling, rainwater harvesting, and sewage treatment plant.

Phase 1 includes approximately 786 residential plots in a meticulously planned layout with dedicated green belts, parks, and open spaces forming a significant portion of the 49.1-acre township.
