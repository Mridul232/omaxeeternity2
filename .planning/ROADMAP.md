# Roadmap: Omaxe Eternity 2 Website Optimization

## Overview

The journey to dominate search engine performance and maximize high-intent lead generation. We will first eliminate technical barriers dragging down the site's organic indexation, and then redesign conversion pathways to capture serious real estate investors frictionlessly.

## Phases

- [ ] **Phase 1: Technical SEO Rescue** - Eliminate `.html` redirect loops and align canonical routing.
- [ ] **Phase 2: CRO & Lead Quality** - Optimize WhatsApp CTAs, Direct Call buttons, and investor targeting copy.

## Phase Details

### Phase 1: Technical SEO Rescue
**Goal**: Resolve all Google Search Console indexation errors related to URL resolution.
**Depends on**: Nothing (first phase)
**Requirements**: SEO-01, SEO-02, SEO-03
**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE):
  1. Internal navigation links (menus, footers, contextual links) do not contain `.html` extensions.
  2. The `sitemap.xml` reflects clean route representations.
  3. `<link rel="canonical">` tags correctly point to the clean route layout without trailing extensions.
**Plans**: 1 plan

Plans:
- [ ] 01-01: Update internal routes, `.xml` generation logic, and global canonical metadata.

### Phase 2: CRO & Lead Quality
**Goal**: Increase conversion velocity and lead intent via optimized communication funnels.
**Depends on**: Phase 1
**Requirements**: CRO-01, CRO-02, CRO-03, LEAD-01
**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE):
  1. WhatsApp and Call buttons are visually sticky and prominent across all screen sizes (especially mobile).
  2. CTA microcopy explicitly captures high-intent actions (e.g., "Request Price List via WhatsApp").
  3. Supporting content pre-qualifies traffic, driving investor-grade leads to the contact pathways.
**Plans**: 2 plans

Plans:
- [ ] 02-01: Redesign and implement sticky, aggressive conversion CTAs for WhatsApp and Phone.
- [ ] 02-02: Revise layout copy near conversion points to enhance investor appeal.
