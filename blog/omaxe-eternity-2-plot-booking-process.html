How to Book a Plot in Omaxe Eternity 2 Vrindavan – Complete 2026 Guide

Step-by-step booking process, documents required, amount, payment plan & registration details

📋 Quick Facts Before Booking

  • Project: Omaxe Eternity 2 Vrindavan – Phase 1
  • RERA No.: UPRERAPRJ141620/12/2025 (Registered 24-Dec-2025)
  • Possession Date: 07 May 2030
  • Developer: Omaxe Limited (BSE & NSE listed, 35+ yrs)
  • Plot Sizes: 120 / 160 / 200 sq. yard
  • Location: Raal Road (Radha Kund Road), Vrindavan, UP – 281121
  • Availability: Limited (750+ of ~786 sold at launch)

Step-by-Step Booking Process (2026)

1

Contact the Authorized Channel Partner

Call or WhatsApp Ashish Garg at +91 94108 56555 to get the latest price list, available plot inventory, and payment plan options. As an authorized channel partner, we provide the most accurate and current information — at no extra cost to you.

2

Schedule a Free Site Visit

We arrange complimentary, guided site visits to Omaxe Eternity 2 on Raal Road, Vrindavan. Site visits are available 7 days a week, 9 AM – 9 PM. See the project layout, roads, parks, and entry gate firsthand before making any commitment.

3

Choose Your Plot Size & Location

Select from 120 sq. yard, 160 sq. yard, or 200 sq. yard. Choose your preferred facing: corner plot, park-facing, main road-facing, or interior. Each comes with different PLC (Preferential Location Charges). We help you pick the best option within your budget.

4

Submit Documents & Pay Booking Amount

Submit required documents (listed below) and pay the booking amount via cheque, DD, or NEFT to Omaxe Limited. This blocks your chosen plot. The amount is typically a percentage of the total plot price.

5

Receive Allotment Letter

Within a few days of booking, Omaxe Limited issues an official Plot Allotment Letter confirming: plot number, block, size, total price, and payment schedule. This is your first official document of ownership.

6

Sign Agreement to Sale (ATS)

Within 30 days of allotment, sign the Agreement to Sale with Omaxe Limited. This legally binding agreement specifies all terms: plot details, total price, payment schedule, possession date (07 May 2030), and developer's obligations under RERA.

7

Pay as per Schedule (Until 2030)

Make subsequent payments as per your chosen plan — either quarterly installments or demand-linked payments. Bank loan EMIs can also be structured here. We track payment schedules on your behalf.

8

Registry & Possession (May 2030)

On possession (May 2030), complete the sub-registrar registry/Sale Deed by paying UP stamp duty (approx. 6-7% of circle rate). Receive the keys and take physical possession of your plot. You can immediately begin construction.

Important 2026 Notes

  • RERA Protection: As a RERA-registered project, any delay in handover beyond May 2030 entitles you to penalty interest from Omaxe Limited
  • UP Stamp Duty: Currently 6% (women buyers may get concession of 1% — UP government policy)
  • GST: Plots are generally exempt from GST under current rules; confirm with tax advisor
  • Bank Loans: SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis Bank offer plot loans. We assist with the loan process
  • NRI Buyers: Can buy plots under FEMA regulations with additional NRI-specific documentation
  • Joint Purchase: Allowed. Both applicants' documents and PAN required
