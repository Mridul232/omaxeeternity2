How is Plot Price Per Sq Yard Calculated in Omaxe Eternity 2?

The total cost of your plot in Omaxe Eternity 2 is based on a base price per sq yard, multiplied by the plot size, plus additional charges. Here's the complete formula:

📐 Total Plot Cost = Base Rate × Plot Area + Additional Charges

  • Base Price per Sq Yard × Plot size (120/160/200 sq yd) = Basic Plot Price
  • + PLC (Preferential Location Charges) for corner/park/road-facing plots
  • + EDC/IDC (External & Internal Development Charges) if applicable
  • + Stamp Duty & Registration (UP state: typically 6–7%)
  • + IFMS (Interest Free Maintenance Security — one time)
  • = Total Cost of Plot

What Are PLC Charges? (Preferential Location Charges)

PLC is an additional premium applied on better-located plots within the township. In Omaxe Eternity 2 Vrindavan, PLC typically applies to:

  • Park-Facing Plots — plots facing a green park or garden area
  • Corner Plots — plots on the corner of two roads (more natural light, bigger frontage)
  • Main Road-Facing — plots on the project's main internal or external roads
  • Wide Road-Facing — plots on the 45m Master Plan Road fetch a premium

💡 Non-PLC plots are available at the base price — ideal for buyers who want to maximize investment returns without paying a premium for location benefits.
