Can NRIs Buy Plots in Vrindavan? (Legal Answer 2026)

Yes — NRIs (Non-Resident Indians) have the full legal right to purchase residential plots in Vrindavan under FEMA (Foreign Exchange Management Act) and RBI guidelines. Here are the key points:

  • NRIs can buy residential and commercial properties in India (except agricultural land/farmhouse)
  • Residential plots in RERA-registered projects like Omaxe Eternity 2 are fully eligible for NRI purchase
  • Payment can be made through NRE/NRO/FCNR accounts or via inward remittance from abroad
  • NRIs do not need RBI permission for property purchase — only basic KYC is required
  • Rental income or proceeds from sale can be repatriated abroad (subject to FEMA limits)

NRI Frequently Asked Questions

🌍 Can an NRI buy a residential plot in Vrindavan?

Yes. Under FEMA guidelines, NRIs are fully eligible to purchase residential plots in India including in Vrindavan. RERA-registered projects like Omaxe Eternity 2 are the safest choice.

💰 Do I need to visit India to buy the plot?

No. The booking, agreement signing, and payment can all be done remotely. For the final registration, you can issue a Power of Attorney to a family member in India who will complete the process on your behalf.

📊 What taxes apply to NRIs buying property in India?

NRIs pay stamp duty and registration charges (as per UP state rates, typically 6-7%). GST may apply on the booking amount. There is no TDS on plot purchase price (applies on rental income). Capital gains tax applies on resale.

🔄 Can NRIs repatriate money after selling the property?

Yes. NRIs can repatriate up to USD 1 million per financial year from proceeds of up to 2 residential properties. The amount must be credited to NRO account first, then transferred via Form 15CA/15CB.
