Project Overview

Omaxe Eternity 2 Vrindavan is a RERA-registered (UPRERAPRJ141620/12/2025) plotted township launched December 2025 by Omaxe Limited on Raal Road, Vrindavan. Phase 1: 49.1 acres, ~786 plots, 120/160/200 sq. yd., possession May 2030. Over 750 units sold at launch.

✅ Pros of Omaxe Eternity 2 Vrindavan

  • ✅ RERA Registered – UP RERA No. UPRERAPRJ141620/12/2025. Full legal protection. Possession date locked at 07 May 2030.
  • ✅ Trusted Developer – Omaxe Limited. BSE & NSE listed. ISO 9001:2015. 35+ years, 130M+ sq. ft. delivered. Phase 1 of Eternity already delivered.
  • ✅ Prime Location – Raal Road, Vrindavan. ~150 km Delhi NCR. Walking distance from ISKCON, Banke Bihari, Prem Mandir temples.
  • ✅ 15-20% Annual Appreciation – Vrindavan's limited land supply + spiritual tourism boom drives consistent price growth.
  • ✅ Modern Township Layout – 45m wide roads, green parks, temple complex, clubhouse, EV charging, gated security.

⚠️ Cons to Consider

  • ⚠️ Possession in 2030 – Long timeline. Ideal for investors; not for those needing immediate occupancy.
  • ⚠️ Very Limited Inventory – 750+ of 786 plots sold at launch. Act fast to get your preferred plot.
